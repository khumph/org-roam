#+title: Zettelkasten method
#+roam_key: https://zettelkasten.de/posts/overview/

Note-taking method. In paper form, uses a "slip-box" or "Zettelkasten" of small cards, each with a single concept on it. The concepts on each card are linked together.

This method is an inspiration for [[file:roam_research.org][Roam Research]] and by extension [[file:org_roam.org][Org-roam]].
